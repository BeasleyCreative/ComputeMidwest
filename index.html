About Compute Midwest

Compute Midwest (CMW) is a conference + hackathon in Kansas City, MO that delves into new ideas, emerging technologies, and especially their impact on the Midwest region looking forward.

You’ll hear from tech leaders, startup founders, and innovators as they share their vision of the future. Find out where technology is going and discover how Kansas City can capitalize on Google Fiber.

Compute Midwest is hosted by Kansas City IT Professionals (KCITP), a tech community of over 9,000.
